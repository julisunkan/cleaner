BODY:

What is image metadata?
Every digital photo carries invisible data called EXIF metadata.
This can include your precise GPS location, the make and model
of your phone or camera, the date and time the photo was taken,
your device's serial number, and the software used to edit it.
When you post a photo online, that data often goes with it.

Who is this for?
— Journalists and activists who need to protect sources
— Real estate sellers who don't want their home's location
  embedded in listing photos
— Privacy-conscious individuals who don't want their device
  fingerprint shared with every image
— Developers and designers who need clean, metadata-free assets
— Anyone who shares photos publicly and values their privacy

What you can do:

  Remove All Metadata
  Wipes every EXIF field — camera info, timestamps, GPS,
  software, copyright, serial numbers — everything.

  GPS Only
  Removes just location data while keeping other metadata
  like camera model and timestamps intact.

  Custom Field Editor
  Choose exactly which fields to remove and which to keep.
  Fine-grained control for advanced users.

  Format Conversion
  Output your cleaned image as JPEG, PNG, or WebP regardless
  of the original format.

  Resize on Clean
  Limit output dimensions (e.g. max 1920px wide) as part of
  the cleaning step.

  Privacy Risk Score
  Every image receives a risk rating — LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH —
  based on what data was found. See at a glance how exposed
  your photo was before cleaning.

  Batch Processing
  Upload and clean dozens of images at once. Download all
  cleaned files in a single ZIP.

  GPS Map View
  See exactly where your photos were taken, plotted on an
  interactive map — before you remove the coordinates.

  Metadata CSV Export
  Download a full before-and-after report of every field
  detected in your image.

  Session History
  Every cleaned image is saved in your browser session for
  re-download. Nothing touches our servers after processing.

  Undo / Get Original
  Changed your mind? Retrieve the original uncleaned file
  from your session at any time.

Privacy guarantee:

them. We do not log your images, store metadata, or track
what you upload. Your privacy is the entire point.
